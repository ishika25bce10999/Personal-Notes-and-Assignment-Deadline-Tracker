# **Personal Notes & Assignment Deadline Tracker**

## ● Problem Statement
Students struggle with deadline management, poor prioritization, and lack of predictive insights for academic task completion.

## ● Scope of the Project
Notes/assignments CRUD, ML risk prediction, automated scheduling, local storage


## ● Target Users
 University and college students
 Graduate researchers, online learners, academic tutors

## ● High-Level Features
- Notes and assignment management with CRUD operations
- ML-powered risk prediction and classification
- Automated work schedule recommendations
- Search, filtering, and data visualization
- Local JSON data persistence
